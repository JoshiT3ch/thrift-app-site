Delete Account

How account deletion works in Thrift.

Users can request or initiate account deletion from within the Thrift mobile app. This page is provided to support user transparency and app store review requirements for account deletion access.

Last updated: April 2026

How to request deletion

If you want to delete your Thrift account, use the account deletion option available inside the mobile app. In general, users should:

  1. Open the Thrift mobile app and sign in to the account you want to delete.
  2. Go to the account or settings area where account management options are available.
  3. Choose the delete account option and confirm the request when prompted.

If you cannot access the app or need help completing the request, contact support@thriftapp.com.

What happens when an account is deleted

Once a deletion request is completed, the following generally happens:

  • Your profile is disabled and removed from normal public visibility.
  • Your active listings are removed from the marketplace.
  • Your saved favorites are deleted or disassociated from the account.
  • Your login access to the deleted account ends.

Messages and moderation records

Some information may be retained after deletion where there is a legitimate need to preserve it. This can include message records, reports, moderation history, safety investigations, fraud-related information, technical logs, and limited backup data.

These retained records are not kept for marketing use. They may be preserved only for reasons such as abuse prevention, dispute handling, legal compliance, security review, or audit needs.

Data retention exceptions

Even after deletion, Thrift may retain certain information if doing so is reasonably necessary to:

  • Investigate fraud, abuse, spam, or counterfeit activity.
  • Document enforcement decisions or protect other users.
  • Comply with legal obligations, lawful requests, or record-keeping requirements.
  • Maintain backup integrity, system security, or limited audit trails.
