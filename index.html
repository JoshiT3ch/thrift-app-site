How it works

Three simple ways the marketplace comes together.

The goal is not to reinvent resale. It is to make secondhand fashion feel easier to browse, easier to trust, and easier to act on.

Buy

Browse what fits your style and budget.

Explore preloved listings, check condition details, compare options, and use search or filters to narrow down size, price, category, and more.

Sell

List items clearly and reach people already looking for thrifted fashion.

Sellers can create listings with photos, pricing, descriptions, and condition notes so buyers can make more informed decisions.

Connect

Coordinate directly, with the details in one place.

Buyers and sellers can chat in-app, send purchase requests, review profiles, and work out meetup or delivery arrangements.

Why Thrift

A more focused home for secondhand clothing.

Thrift is shaped around the kind of resale experience people actually want when they are looking for clothing, accessories, and wardrobe basics. It is intentionally direct, with an emphasis on clarity, trust, and community behavior.

  • Community-driven, so buyers and sellers build the marketplace together.
  • Affordable fashion access through resale, reuse, and practical discovery.
  • Aligned with re-commerce and circular fashion instead of disposable consumption.
  • Simple enough to move quickly without losing the safeguards people expect.

Trust and safety

Moderation matters when the platform is built around people.

Trust is not an add-on. Thrift includes reporting tools, moderation workflows, and seller accountability features intended to reduce abuse and protect the marketplace over time.

Reporting

Users can report listings, messages, and behavior that appears unsafe, deceptive, abusive, or otherwise out of bounds.

Moderation

Listings and user activity may be reviewed to enforce platform rules, investigate complaints, and respond to misuse.

Seller accountability

Profile information, ratings, and enforcement history all support a healthier marketplace environment.

Community guidelines

Clear conduct expectations help keep conversations respectful and listings honest and lawful.
